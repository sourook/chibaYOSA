SUGGOI WORLD!

Completed in 2015, “SUGGOI WORLD!” was created as a copyright-free dance to be enjoyed at festivals, YOSAKOI events, and sports days across Japan.

The choreography was supervised by Mr. Takeshi Miyamoto, known for the popular dance “Yocchore.” YOSAKOI choreographers from Chiba collaborated on this project, focusing on the theme “easy and fun for anyone to dance.”

The audio, choreography video, and lyrics are all released as copyright-free. Feel free to use or share this page without restriction.
