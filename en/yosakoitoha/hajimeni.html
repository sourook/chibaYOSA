About ChibaYOSAKOI

The city is the stage, and passion resonates

Introduction

ChibaYOSAKOI was first held in 2004 to enliven the city of Chiba through "Yosakoi," a festival where people of all ages can enjoy and the entire city becomes the stage.

Even after cancellations and venue changes due to economic downturns and bad weather,
the festival has continued to this day, supported by the passion for Yosakoi.

As a festival that “seriously determines the top”, we place great importance on fair judging.
The only rule is "hold naruko in your hands." Choreography, costumes, and music are all free.
Performances that make full use of this freedom captivate the audience and deliver emotion.

Supported by few rules and many manners, chibayosaKOI aims to deliver "fun" and "emotion" to many people through festival management.
